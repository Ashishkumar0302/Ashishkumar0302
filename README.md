## Building AI systems that move from notebooks to usable products

I work across **agentic AI, retrieval-augmented generation, machine learning pipelines, and full-stack AI applications**. My projects focus on practical systems: research agents, ranking models, forecasting pipelines, domain-specific AI assistants, and data products built from messy real-world datasets.

Currently, my strongest work is around **Deep Research agents**, **multi-agent RAG systems**, and **data science challenge solutions**.
